How LAES Works

Liquid Air Energy Storage (LAES) ensures 24/7 clean energy by storing excess power from renewables like wind and solar. Scalable, long-lasting, and eco-friendly, it reduces emissions, stabilizes the grid, and minimizes reliance on fossil fuels. LAES is a key solution for powering a sustainable planet.

1. Compression

Ambient air is compressed in stages and cooled.

2. Liquefaction

Air is liquefied at -196°C and stored cryogenically.

3. Storage

Stored in insulated tanks until needed.

4. Regasification

Expanded through turbines to generate power.

Why Choose LAES?

Environmentally Friendly

Uses ambient air with no hazardous materials.

Long Lifespan

Operates efficiently for 40-50 years.

Grid Resilience

Supports renewable energy integration and peak management.

No Geographical Constraints

LAES is not limited by terrain or geology like pumped hydro or CAES, allowing flexible siting.

Long Lifespan and Scalable

With a 40-50 year lifespan and modular design, LAES is fit for both local and grid-scale needs.

Integration with Waste Heat Recovery

LAES systems boost efficiency by recovering industrial waste heat or cold energy streams.

Environmentally Friendly and Safe
